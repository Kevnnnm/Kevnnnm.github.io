About Me!

Hello! I'm Squirtle, a water type Pokemon from Generation 1
I am a small blue turtle that sprays water at my enemies.
I'm weak against grass and electric type pokemon but strong against ground, rock, and fire type pokemon
Some of my famous moves include:

  • Skull Bash
  • Bubble Beam
  • Aqua Tail
  • Aqua Jet

When I get stronger, I evolve into Wartortle, then Blastoise, and in some cases, Mega Blastoise!
